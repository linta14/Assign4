About Our Firm’s



Fresh meat shops is fresh meat & poultry specialty store delivering a variety of chicken, meat & beef cuts. Our products are 100% fresh not frozen, we deliver only fresh meat to our customers,our primary objective is to deliver only fresh products to our customers without any chemical, water or freezing treatment. Our meat is slaughtered by professional butchers, and provide special cuts like pasanday, handmade mince, lollipops, chicken steak, beef T bone steak etc. Our fresh meat give your food taste, which frozen meat can’t deliver. Our delivery time starts early in the morning in order to match your cooking time, and give your food fresh meat. Together with meat, we also deliver yogurt, egg & bread, so that you can cook your food with fresh ingredients.
